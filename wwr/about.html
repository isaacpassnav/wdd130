History

Rafting, that thrilling activity that challenges turbulent waters, has its roots in the human need to explore the unknown and conquer the wild. It dates back to ancient times when civilizations relied on rivers for trade, agriculture, and survival. The history of modern rafting traces back to the picturesque mountain regions of North America, where brave explorers and pioneers ventured into the rapids of unknown rivers. In the 1970s, rafting emerged as a recreational activity, attracting intrepid adventurers in search of excitement and camaraderie amidst the wild.

Since then, rafting has evolved into an exhilarating sport and a popular form of outdoor adventure worldwide. Today, people of all ages and abilities can enjoy the thrilling challenge of paddling through tumultuous rapids, surrounded by stunning landscapes and the thrill of rushing water. At our rafting center, we take pride in being part of this passionate history. With expert guides, high-quality gear, and a commitment to safety and fun, we invite you to join us on an adventure that will take you down the same watery paths once traveled by bold pioneers. Come and join us as we explore wild waters, create unforgettable memories, and continue writing together the story of rafting.
